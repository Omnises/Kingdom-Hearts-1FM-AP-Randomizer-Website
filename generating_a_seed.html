Software Setup | Generating a Seed | Generating the Mod Zip | Installing the Generated Mod

  • Navigate to the seed generation page
  • Expand the categories found there to adjust your desired settings.
  • When you have adjusted the settings to your liking, click "Generate Settings File".

  • This will prompt you to save your settings file. This file is what is used to generate seeds with those settings.
  • At the bottom of the page, upload your settings file and click "Generate Seed"

  • This will prompt you to download your seed zip, which is unique to that seed and used in the next step, Generating the mod zip
  • You'll also see a seed link appear. If you plan on playing offline, and you set your "Remote Items" settings to "off", you can ignore this link.
  • Otherwise, you can use this link to host a server on the Archipelago website to connect to in order to use remote items.
