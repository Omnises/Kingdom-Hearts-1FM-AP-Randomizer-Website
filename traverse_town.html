Traverse Town Item Shop Postcard Item Shop In the Item Shop, with Huey, Dewey, and Louie, hit the ceiling fan with the keyblade.
Traverse Town Accessory Shop Chest Accessory Shop Open the chest near the entrance door on top of a cabinet.
Traverse Town 1st District Accessory Shop Roof Chest 1st District Open the chest on top of the central building in the 1st District.
This does not require any items. Moving a nearby crate or parkouring from the other side of the roof grants access to this chest.
Traverse Town Mail Postcard Event 1st District Interact with the mailbox with the funny hat to turn in collected postcards.
The mailbox will give 10 items total, one for each postcard turned in. It will give the rewards sequentially.
Traverse Town 1st District Blue Trinity Balcony Chest 1st District Open the chest in the balcony above the candles and item shop.
This requires either BLUE TRINITY or GLIDE.
Both are required logically, in case the Blue Trinity is activated by the chest is not opened. With Advanced Logic enabled, only GLIDE is expected.
Traverse Town 2nd District Boots and Shoes Awning Chest 2nd District Open the chest on an awning to the right entering from the 1st District.
It can be jumped to via a nearby lantern.
Traverse Town 2nd District Gizmo Shop Facade Chest 2nd District Open the chest on the facade between the two entrances of the Gizmo Shop.
This can be reached with a tight jump and keyblade swing, or any movement ability.
Alternatively, this chest can be reached by falling from the Gizmo Shop roof by climbing the ladder after defeating Guard Armor.
Traverse Town 2nd District Rooftop Chest 2nd District Open the chest on top of the roof above the Boots and Shoes awning chest.
This can be reached across from the Gizmo Shop roof, or with a well placed High Jump.
Traverse Town Piano Room Puppies Dalmatian House Walk into the box left after the cutscene with Pongo and Perdita in the Piano Room, accessible via the 2nd District or the Alleyway.
The rewards are only given by collecting certain amount of puppies. Rewards are given at:
10 Puppies, 20 Puppies, 30 Puppies, 40 Puppies, two rewards at 50 Puppies, 60 Puppies, 70 Puppies, 80 Puppies, 90 Puppies, and two rewards at 99 Puppies.
Traverse Town Alleyway Blue Room Awning Chest Alleyway >Open the chest by the last balcony at the opposite end of the Waterway entrance.
Traverse Town Alleyway Corner Chest Alleyway Open the chest tucked away in the corner nearby the Balcony Chest.
Traverse Town Alleyway Balcony Chest Alleyway Open the chest on the balcony on the opposite side of Blue Room Awning Chest and the Corner Chest, by the Waterway entrance.
Traverse Town Secret Waterway Near Stairs Chest Secret Waterway Open the chest at the bottom of the stairway that would lead to the Mystical House. The waterway is opened in Randomizer.
Traverse Town Green Room Table Chest Green Room Open the chest on the table in the room, first available after the fight with Leon.
You must talk with Yuffie or Leon first before the chest can be opened.
Traverse Town Green Room Clock Puzzle Chest Green Room Open the chest that appears on top of a shelf after hitting the clock above the door to the Red Room.
It's finnicky and precise, and must but hit quite a few times. You will hear two jingles, after the second the chest will appear. The chest does not go away if the clock is hit more.
Traverse Town Red Room Chest Red Room Open the chest by the door leading to the Alleyway.
